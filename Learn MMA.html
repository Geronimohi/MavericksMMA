FUNDAMENTALS OF FIGHTING

Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) is a combat sport that demands a unique blend of skills, combining elements from various fighting disciplines into a singular, dynamic battlefield. In the cage, fighters face a multitude of challenges, from striking to grappling, and everything in between. To excel in this intense and physically demanding sport, one must grasp the essential fundamentals of fighting specific to MMA. Join us as we delve into the intricacies of MMA combat, exploring the core principles and techniques that make champions in this unforgiving arena. Whether you're a seasoned fighter, an aspiring athlete, or a passionate spectator, understanding the fundamentals is the first step in unlocking the secrets to success in MMA.




























Basic Striking
Footwork, Movement, Breathing and Basics to using your fists.
Advanced Striking
Feints, Head Movement, Setups, Clever Positioning and Kicks
Grappling
Takedowns, Positions, Hand Grips, Ground-and-pound, and Submissions
